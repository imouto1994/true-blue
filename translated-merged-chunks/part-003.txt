“...This show is actually pretty damn good.”
After my bath, I wasn't quite ready for bed, so I was in the living room watching TV.
I hadn't decided on anything specific to watch; I figured if I turned it on and there was nothing but boring trash, I’d just shut it off immediately.
But that thought had vanished into the distance.
It was a drama everyone in class had been talking about, and just like the rumors said, it was surprisingly compelling.
The protagonists were a lanky male detective and a fairly beautiful female assistant.
The premise was the two of them using science to debunk various occult phenomena.
Well, it was basically your typical detective show, but it was an ambitious work that skillfully wove in those supernatural elements.
Despite being broadcast in a late-night slot, it now boasted immense popularity across a wide range of viewers.
So far, three series existed, and what was airing now was actually a rerun of the very first season.
Apparently, to coincide with a movie coming out this winter, they were airing all three series back-to-back.
I’m not usually the type to watch dramas, but this one was interesting.
Between the comedy-duo banter of the two leads and the visual tricks the supporting cast occasionally pulled, there were plenty of laughs to be had.
$Akito
“Man... I think I’m getting hooked.”

“What? ...At this hour...?”
